Analyze the given research area to identify unexplored research gaps and opportunities.

$ARGUMENTS

## Analysis Process

1. **Landscape Mapping**: Identify the top 10 most influential papers in this area (by citation or impact)

2. **Categorize Approaches**: Group existing work into 3-5 methodological categories. For each:
   - What has been well-explored?
   - What assumptions do most papers share?

3. **Identify Gaps**: Look for:
   - Combinations of methods that haven't been tried
   - Domains or datasets that are underexplored
   - Assumptions that could be relaxed or challenged
   - Scaling limitations that haven't been addressed
   - Cross-disciplinary connections that are missing

4. **Rank Opportunities**: For each gap, rate:
   - Feasibility (1-5): How easy is it to tackle?
   - Impact (1-5): How significant would a solution be?
   - Novelty (1-5): How novel would this contribution be?

5. **Recommend**: Select the top 3 most promising research directions. For each, draft a 2-sentence research proposal.
