## Review Structure

1. **Summary** (3-4 sentences): What is the paper about? What problem does it solve?

2. **Strengths** (bullet points):
   - What does the paper do well?
   - What are novel contributions?
   - Is the evaluation convincing?

3. **Weaknesses** (bullet points):
   - What are the limitations?
   - Are there methodological concerns?
   - What is missing from the evaluation?

4. **Questions for Authors**:
   - List 3-5 clarifying questions you would ask

5. **Overall Assessment**:
   - Significance: Low / Medium / High
   - Novelty: Low / Medium / High
   - Clarity: Low / Medium / High
   - Recommendation: Accept / Weak Accept / Borderline / Weak Reject / Reject

6. **Suggestions for Improvement**:
   - 2-3 concrete suggestions to strengthen the work
