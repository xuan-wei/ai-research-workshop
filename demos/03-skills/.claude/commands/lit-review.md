Conduct a systematic literature review on the given topic.

$ARGUMENTS

## Steps

1. **Define Scope**: Identify 3-5 key search terms related to the topic
2. **Search**: For each search term, find 5-10 relevant papers (prioritize recent, high-citation papers)
3. **Summarize**: Create a summary table with columns:
   - Paper Title
   - Authors (First Author et al.)
   - Year
   - Venue
   - Key Contribution
   - Method Category
   - Limitations
4. **Synthesize**: Write a 2-3 paragraph synthesis identifying:
   - Main research themes and trends
   - Dominant methodological approaches
   - Key open problems and research gaps
5. **Suggest**: Recommend 3 promising research directions based on the gaps identified
